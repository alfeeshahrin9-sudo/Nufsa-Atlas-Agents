# 🕵️‍♂️ Nufsa Atlas Agents

Welcome to **Nufsa Atlas Agents**, an immersive, retro-inspired pixel tile art hidden object game. Step into the shoes of a brilliant detective, sharpen your observation skills, and journey across beautifully crafted tilemaps to track down elusive items and solve mysteries.

Built with a modern web tech stack (**Vite**, **TypeScript**, and **HTML5 Canvas**), this project showcases precise tile grid logic, smooth interactive tracking, and crisp pixel-art styling.

---

## 🗺️ Explore the Worlds

Put your detective instincts to the test across **three unique, custom-designed pixel-art maps**:

1. **🏙️ The Bustling Town**
   * *The Brief:* Navigating crowded city streets, lively marketplaces, and intricate alleyways.
   * *Challenge:* Filter out the chaos of urban life to spot everyday objects cleverly hidden in plain sight.

2. **🍂 The Golden Autumn**
   * *The Brief:* Wandering through a peaceful yet mysterious landscape covered in falling orange leaves, cozy cabins, and rustic parks.
   * *Challenge:* Distinguish hidden secrets masked by seasonal colors and organic foliage shadows.

3. **🏜️ The Scorching Desert**
   * *The Brief:* Traversing vast sand dunes, barren canyons, ancient ruins, and isolated oases.
   * *Challenge:* Spot rare relics and obscured tracks blending seamlessly into the arid environment.

---

## 🕹️ Key Features

* **Grid-Based Detective Lens:** Click or tap precisely on pixel tiles to investigate areas and identify target clues.
* **Dynamic Clue Ledger:** Track your progress with a clean UI showing which hidden objects remain in the current area.
* **Immersive Visual Style:** Authentic pixel-art scaling that preserves sharp textures, grid layouts, and custom-layered tile designs.
* **Lightweight & High-Performance:** Zero bloat—rendering utilizes lightweight frontend patterns for instantaneous map loading and click detection.
